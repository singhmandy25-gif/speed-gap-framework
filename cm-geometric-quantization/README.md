Summary
N identical spheres merging with volume conservation give surface ratio N^(−1/3) and compression W = 1/N². Since N is an integer, energy levels are discrete — quantization from counting, not from wave mechanics. The identity N² = D+1 singles out D = 3 as the only dimension where pair merge (N = 2) creates confinement. Energy = density × volume gives E_n = 1/n², with the general formula E_n ∝ n^(−(2D−4)) showing stable bound states exist only for D = 3. Verified across 6 independent domains. Zero free parameters.
